| Method     | Description                    | Example              | Output     |
| ---------- | ------------------------------ | -------------------- | ---------- |
| `strip()`  | Removes spaces from both sides | `" hello ".strip()`  | `'hello'`  |
| `lstrip()` | Removes spaces from left side  | `" hello ".lstrip()` | `'hello '` |
| `rstrip()` | Removes spaces from right side | `" hello ".rstrip()` | `' hello'` |
