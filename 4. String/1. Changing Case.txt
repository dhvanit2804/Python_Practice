| `lower()`      | Converts to lowercase                  | `"HELLO".lower()`         | `'hello'`         |
| `title()`      | Capitalizes first letter of each word  | `"python is fun".title()` | `'Python Is Fun'` |
| `capitalize()` | Capitalizes first letter of the string | `"python".capitalize()`   | `'Python'`        |
